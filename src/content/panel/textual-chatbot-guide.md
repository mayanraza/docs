# Textual Chatbot Creation Guide

Create a powerful website chatbot in 6 easy steps. Follow this guide to get your AI assistant live on your site.

---

## Step 1: Connect Site

![Connect Site](../../images/panel.botifire.com/textaual%20cahtbots%20creation%20steps/Step%201%20(crawling).png)

### What happens here?
Your website gets scanned automatically to teach the chatbot about your business.

### Quick Setup

**Website URL**  
Enter your site address: `https://yoursite.com`

**Scan Full Page**  
Turn this ON to scan your entire website

**Content Categories**  
The system finds different sections on your site (About, Services, Projects, etc.). Check the boxes for sections you want your chatbot to learn from.

> **Pro Tip:** Less is more! Only select categories with useful customer information.

---

## Step 2: Greeting Builder

![Greeting Builder](../../images/panel.botifire.com/textaual%20cahtbots%20creation%20steps/step%202%20(greeting%20builder).png)

### What happens here?
Create the first message visitors see when they open your chatbot.

### Quick Setup

**Welcome Message**  
Write a friendly greeting (e.g., "Hey! I'm here to help with any questions about our services!")

**Quick Buttons**  
Add 3-5 buttons for common questions:
- FAQ
- About Us  
- Contact
- Pricing

**Company Info**  
- Your business name
- Industry type
- Contact number & email

**Social Media** (Optional)  
Link your Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, etc.

> **Pro Tip:** Keep your greeting short and tell users exactly what you can help with.

---

## Step 3: Knowledge Base

![Knowledge Base](../../images/panel.botifire.com/textaual%20cahtbots%20creation%20steps/step%203%20(knowledge%20base).png)

### What happens here?
Add extra content to make your chatbot smarter.

### Add Content

**Add URL** - Import from web pages  
**Add Files** - Upload PDFs, docs  
**Create Text** - Write custom Q&A

**Support Articles**  
See all content you've added. You can edit or remove items anytime.

> **Pro Tip:** Add your most frequently asked questions here for best results.

---

## Step 4: Style Studio

![Style Studio](../../images/panel.botifire.com/textaual%20cahtbots%20creation%20steps/step%204%20(style%20studio).png)

### What happens here?
Make your chatbot match your brand.

### Customize Look

**Logo** - Upload your company logo  
**Colors** - Pick your brand color  
**Position** - Left or right corner

### Choose Template

Pick a style that fits your brand:
- **WhatsApp** - Green, familiar
- **Messenger** - Blue, professional  
- **Instagram** - Dark, modern
- **Snapchat** - Yellow, playful

Each has 4 layout options.

> **Pro Tip:** Match your website colors for a seamless look.

---

## Step 5: Integration

![Integration](../../images/panel.botifire.com/textaual%20cahtbots%20creation%20steps/step%205%20(integration).png)

### What happens here?
Get your chatbot live on your website.

### Install in 3 Steps

1. **Copy** the code snippet provided
2. **Paste** into your website's HTML (before `</head>` or `</body>`)
3. **Save** and your chatbot appears

### Platform Quick Guide

| Platform | Where to Paste |
|----------|----------------|
| **WordPress** | Use Header/Footer plugin |
| **Shopify** | theme.liquid file |
| **Wix/Squarespace** | Custom code section |
| **HTML Site** | Directly in HTML |

> **Pro Tip:** The code won't slow down your site - it loads asynchronously.

---

## Step 6: Persona and Tone

![Persona and Tone](../../images/panel.botifire.com/textaual%20cahtbots%20creation%20steps/step%206%20(persona%20and%20tone).png)

### What happens here?
Define how your chatbot talks to customers.

### Choose Personality

**Professional** - Formal, business-like  
**Friendly** - Warm, approachable (most popular)  
**Expert** - Knowledgeable, detailed  
**Casual** - Very informal, fun

### Fine-tune

**Formality** - Slide from formal to casual  
**Response Length** - Short, medium, or detailed  
**Custom Rules** - Add specific instructions

> **Pro Tip:** Match your chatbot's tone to your brand voice.
