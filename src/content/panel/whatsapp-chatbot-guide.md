# WhatsApp Chatbot Creation Guide

Create a WhatsApp chatbot in 7 simple steps. Get your AI assistant live on WhatsApp.

---

## Step 1: WABA Setup

![WABA Setup](/images/panel.botifire.com/whatsapp_cahtbots_creation_steps/step_1_havingwabaandnumber.png)

### What happens here?
Connect your WhatsApp Business API to enable messaging.

### What You Need

Get these from **Meta Business Manager**:

| Field | What It Is |
|-------|------------|
| **WABA ID** | Your WhatsApp Business Account number |
| **Meta Number ID** | Your WhatsApp phone number ID |
| **Business ID** | Meta Business Manager account ID |
| **System Token** | Authentication key (keep secure) |
| **APP ID** | Meta app identifier |
| **App Secret** | Security key for webhooks |

### Don't Have WABA?

Select **"I don't have WABA"** and follow the setup wizard to create one through Meta.

> **Pro Tip:** You need a dedicated phone number - can't use regular WhatsApp.

## Step 2: Connect Site

![Connect Site](/images/panel.botifire.com/whatsapp_cahtbots_creation_steps/step_2_connectsite.png)

### What happens here?
Scan your website so your WhatsApp bot learns about your business.

### Quick Setup

**Website URL** - Your site address  
**Scan Full Page** - Turn ON to scan everything  
**Categories** - Select relevant sections (Services, About, Pricing, etc.)

> **Pro Tip:** WhatsApp users want quick answers - focus on essential content.

---

## Step 3: Greeting Builder

![Greeting Builder](/images/panel.botifire.com/whatsapp_cahtbots_creation_steps/step_3_greetingbuilder.png)

### What happens here?
Create your welcome message for WhatsApp.

### Quick Setup

**Welcome Message**  
Keep it casual and friendly.

**Quick Buttons** (Max 3)  
WhatsApp limits you to 3 buttons - choose wisely.

**Company Details**  
- Business name
- Industry
- Contact info

> **Pro Tip:** WhatsApp is casual - be friendly and conversational.

---

## Step 4: Knowledge Base

![Knowledge Base](/images/panel.botifire.com/whatsapp_cahtbots_creation_steps/step_4_knowledgebase.png)

### What happens here?
Add extra content to make your bot smarter.

### Add Content

**URLs** - Import from web pages  
**Files** - Upload PDFs (bot can send these to users)  
**Text** - Write custom answers

> **Pro Tip:** Keep it concise - WhatsApp users are on mobile.

---

## Step 5: Style Studio

![Style Studio](/images/panel.botifire.com/whatsapp_cahtbots_creation_steps/step_5_stylestudio.png)

### What happens here?
Set up your WhatsApp Business Profile.

### Profile Settings

**Profile Picture** - Your logo (shows on all messages)  
**Description** - Short business intro (256 chars max)  
**Business Hours** - When you're available  
**Address** - Your location (optional)

### Bot Behavior

**Response Style** - Concise, balanced, or detailed  
**Emoji Usage** - How many emojis to use  

> **Pro Tip:** Complete profile builds more trust with customers.

---

## Step 6: Integration

![Integration](/images/panel.botifire.com/whatsapp_cahtbots_creation_steps/step_6_integration.png)

### What happens here?
Get your bot live and ready for customers.

### You'll Get

**QR Code** - Print or display for easy scanning  
**WhatsApp Link** - Use on website, emails, social media  
**Status Check** - Confirms everything's connected

### Test Your Bot

1. Save your WhatsApp number
2. Send a test message
3. Check the greeting appears
4. Ask a few questions
5. Verify answers are correct

> **Pro Tip:** Test thoroughly before going public.

---

## Step 7: Persona and Tone

![Persona and Tone](/images/panel.botifire.com/whatsapp_cahtbots_creation_steps/step_7_personaandtone.png)

### What happens here?
Define how your bot communicates on WhatsApp.

### Choose Style

| Personality | Best For |
|-------------|----------|
| **Friendly** | Most businesses (recommended) |
| **Professional** | Finance, legal, healthcare |
| **Casual** | Youth brands, lifestyle |

### Settings

**Formality** - Casual to formal  
**Length** - Short to detailed  
**Emojis** - None to frequent (moderate recommended)

### Custom Instructions

Add rules like:
- "Always mention free shipping"
- "Be enthusiastic about new products"
- "Escalate complaints to humans"

> **Pro Tip:** WhatsApp is casual - don't be too formal.

---

## Step 8: Access Flow Builder

![Go to Flow](/images/panel.botifire.com/whatapp_flow/step_1_(go_to__flow).png)

### What happens here?
Once your chatbot is created, you need to design the conversation logic. Navigate to the **"Flow"** section from your dashboard to start building your bot's automated responses.

### Key Actions
- Click on the **Flow** button on your chatbot card.
- This takes you to the visual builder where the magic happens.

> **Pro Tip:** The flow is the "brain" of your bot - it decides what to say and when.

---

## Step 9: Flow Templates

![Flow Templates](/images/panel.botifire.com/whatapp_flow/step_2_(flow_templates).png)

### What happens here?
You don't have to start from scratch. Choose from a variety of pre-designed templates tailored for different business needs.

### Options
- **Pre-built Templates:** Select a template (like E-commerce, Lead Gen) and customize it.
- **Start Fresh:** Create a unique flow from a blank canvas.

> **Pro Tip:** Templates are the fastest way to get a professional bot up and running.

---

## Step 10: Design with Live Preview

![Design Flow](/images/panel.botifire.com/whatapp_flow/step_3_(flow_with_live_preview).png)

### What happens here?
Use the drag-and-drop editor to build your conversation. Add messages, buttons, and delays while seeing exactly how they will look on a phone.

### Builder Features
- **Drag & Drop Nodes:** Connect messages, images, and interactive buttons.
- **Set Delays:** Make the bot feel more human by adding natural pauses between messages.
- **Live Preview:** Use the phone mockup on the right to test the experience in real-time.

> **Pro Tip:** Keep your initial message short and offer clear choices using buttons.

---

## Step 11: Publish & Go Live

![Publish Flow](/images/panel.botifire.com/whatapp_flow/step_4_(publish).PNG)

### What happens here?
After designing and testing your flow in the previewer, it's time to make it live for your customers.

### Final Steps
- Click the **Publish** button in the top right corner.
- Your WhatsApp chatbot is now officially active with its new flow!

> **Pro Tip:** You can always come back and update your flow even after it's published.

---
