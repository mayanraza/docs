# Step 5: Style Studio

Configure the visual and branding elements for your WhatsApp chatbot. While WhatsApp has a standardized interface, you can still customize your business profile and certain presentation elements.

---

## Overview

The Style Studio for WhatsApp chatbots focuses on your WhatsApp Business Profile settings and how your chatbot presents information to users.

## WhatsApp Business Profile Settings

### Profile Picture
**Purpose:** Your business logo or brand image that appears in WhatsApp.

- Upload a square image (recommended: 640x640 pixels)
- Should be your company logo or recognizable brand image
- Appears next to all messages from your chatbot
- Must comply with WhatsApp Business policies

**Best Practices:**
- Use high-quality images
- Ensure logo is clear at small sizes
- Avoid text-heavy images
- Use your official brand logo

### Business Description
**Purpose:** Short description of your business shown in your WhatsApp profile.

- Maximum 256 characters
- Describe what your business does
- Include key services or products
- Example: "TechBackers - Premium Web & App Development. We build stunning digital experiences for businesses worldwide. ðŸš€"

### Business Category
**Purpose:** Classify your business type.

- Select from WhatsApp's predefined categories
- Examples: Retail, Professional Services, Technology, Healthcare
- Helps users understand your business at a glance
- May affect how WhatsApp displays your profile

### Business Hours
**Purpose:** Let customers know when you're available.

- Set your operating hours
- Specify different hours for different days
- Can show "Open 24/7" if applicable
- Helps manage customer expectations

### Business Address
**Purpose:** Physical location of your business (if applicable).

- Enter your full business address
- Appears in your WhatsApp Business profile
- Users can tap to view on maps
- Optional for online-only businesses

### Business Website
**Purpose:** Link to your website from WhatsApp profile.

- Enter your full website URL
- Users can tap to visit your site
- Should match the URL you used in Step 2
- Example: https://techbackers.io

## Message Formatting Preferences

### Response Style
**Purpose:** Control how the chatbot formats responses.

Options may include:

#### Concise Mode
- Short, direct answers
- Minimal formatting
- Best for quick queries
- Example: "Our pricing starts at $500."

#### Detailed Mode

- Example: Multiple messages with full explanations

### Emoji Usage
**Purpose:** Control emoji frequency in responses.

- **None:** Professional, no emojis
- **Minimal:** Occasional emojis for emphasis
- **Moderate:** Regular emoji use (recommended)
- **Frequent:** Emoji-rich, very casual

**Recommendation:** Moderate for most businesses

## Rich Media Settings

### Image Responses
**Purpose:** Allow chatbot to send images when relevant.

- **Enabled:** Chatbot can send product images, diagrams, infographics
- **Disabled:** Text-only responses
- **Recommendation:** Enable for e-commerce and visual products

### Document Sharing
**Purpose:** Allow chatbot to send PDF files and documents.

- **Enabled:** Can send catalogs, brochures, forms
- **Disabled:** No file sharing
- **Recommendation:** Enable for businesses with downloadable resources

### Location Sharing
**Purpose:** Allow chatbot to send location pins.

- **Enabled:** Can share store locations, office addresses
- **Disabled:** No location sharing
- **Recommendation:** Enable for businesses with physical locations

## Best Practices

> [!TIP]
> **Profile Optimization:**
> - Use a professional, high-quality profile picture
> - Write a clear, concise business description
> - Keep business hours updated
> - Add your website and address for credibility

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **WhatsApp Policies:**
> - Follow WhatsApp Business Policy guidelines
> - Don't use misleading information
> - Keep profile information accurate and current
> - Respect user privacy and data protection

> [!NOTE]
> **Response Formatting:**
> - WhatsApp users prefer concise messages
> - Use emojis to make messages scannable
> - Break long responses into multiple messages
> - Use formatting (*bold*, _italic_) sparingly

## Industry-Specific Recommendations

### E-commerce
- **Profile:** Product catalog image
- **Style:** Moderate emojis, image-rich
- **Features:** Enable image and document sharing
- **Templates:** Order confirmations, shipping updates

### Professional Services
- **Profile:** Company logo
- **Style:** Minimal emojis, concise responses
- **Features:** Document sharing for proposals
- **Templates:** Appointment confirmations

### Hospitality
- **Profile:** Property or brand image
- **Style:** Frequent emojis, friendly tone
- **Features:** Location sharing, images
- **Templates:** Booking confirmations, check-in reminders

### Healthcare
- **Profile:** Clinic/hospital logo
- **Style:** Professional, minimal emojis
- **Features:** Document sharing for forms
- **Templates:** Appointment reminders, test results
