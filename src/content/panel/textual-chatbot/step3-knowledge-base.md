# Step 3: Knowledge Base

Build your chatbot's intelligence by adding documents, URLs, and custom text content. The Knowledge Base is where your chatbot learns the information it needs to answer user questions accurately.

---

## Overview

The Knowledge Base allows you to upload various types of content that your chatbot will use to generate responses. You can add URLs, upload files, or create custom text entries.

![Knowledge Base Step](../../../images/panel.botifire.com/textaual%20cahtbots%20creation%20steps/step%203%20(knowledge%20base).png)

---

## Adding Content to Knowledge Base

### Add URL
**Purpose:** Import content directly from web pages.

- Click the **globe icon** to add URLs
- Enter any publicly accessible webpage URL
- The system will extract and index the content
- Great for documentation, blog posts, or product pages

### Add Files
**Purpose:** Upload documents containing information for your chatbot.

- Click the **document icon** to upload files
- Supported formats: PDF, TXT, DOCX, and more
- Upload product catalogs, manuals, FAQs, or policy documents
- Files are processed and indexed automatically

### Create Text
**Purpose:** Manually enter custom information or Q&A pairs.

- Click the **text icon** to create custom entries
- Write specific answers to common questions
- Add information not available elsewhere
- Useful for company-specific details or policies

---

## Search Knowledge Base

**Purpose:** Quickly find and manage content you've already added.

- Use the search bar to locate specific entries
- Filter through your knowledge base content
- Helps you avoid duplicate entries
- Makes it easy to update or remove outdated information

### Filter Button
- Click **+ Filter** to apply advanced filtering options
- Sort by content type, date added, or source
- Organize large knowledge bases efficiently

## Best Practices

> [!TIP]
> **Quality Over Quantity:** Focus on adding high-quality, relevant content rather than overwhelming the system with too much information. Well-organized, focused content leads to better chatbot responses.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Keep Content Updated:** Regularly review and update your knowledge base to ensure your chatbot provides current and accurate information. Remove outdated content that might confuse users.

> [!NOTE]
> **Processing Time:** After adding content, the system needs a few moments to process and index it. Large files or many URLs may take longer to process.

---

## Content Strategy Tips

1. **Start with FAQs:** Add your most frequently asked questions first
2. **Include Product Details:** Upload product specifications, features, and pricing
3. **Add Company Policies:** Include return policies, shipping info, terms of service
4. **Cover Common Issues:** Document troubleshooting steps and common problems
5. **Update Regularly:** Set a schedule to review and refresh content monthly
