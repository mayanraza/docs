# Step 4: Style Studio

Customize the visual appearance of your chatbot widget to match your brand identity. The Style Studio offers extensive design options including themes, colors, logos, and layout templates.

---

## Overview

Make your chatbot widget visually appealing and on-brand. Choose from pre-designed templates or create a custom look that perfectly matches your website's design.

![Style Studio Step](../../../images/panel.botifire.com/textaual%20cahtbots%20creation%20steps/step%204%20(style%20studio).png)

---

## Widget Theme Settings

### Default Theme
**Purpose:** Choose the base visual style for your chat widget.

Located at the top of the configuration panel, this section controls the overall appearance.

### Show Logo on Chat
**Purpose:** Display your company logo in the chat widget header.

- Toggle ON to show your logo
- Toggle OFF for a cleaner, logo-free header
- Recommended: Keep ON for brand recognition

### Upload Chat Logo
**Purpose:** Add your company logo to the chat interface.

- Click to upload your logo image
- Supported formats: PNG, JPG, SVG
- Recommended size: 200x200 pixels
- Appears in the chat widget header

---

## Color Customization

### Background Color
**Purpose:** Set the primary color scheme for your chat widget.

- Click the color picker to choose your brand color
- This affects the widget header and accent elements
- Default shown: Purple/violet theme
- Choose colors that match your website design

---

## Widget Position

**Purpose:** Control where the chat widget appears on your website.

Choose from multiple position options:
- **Left** - Bottom left corner of the screen
- **Right** - Bottom right corner of the screen (most common)

The widget icon will appear in the selected corner, allowing users to open the chat interface.

---

## WhatsApp Button Template

**Purpose:** Choose from pre-designed chat interface templates inspired by popular messaging platforms.

The Style Studio offers multiple template designs to choose from:

### Template Options

#### 1. **WhatsApp Style** (Green Theme)
- Classic WhatsApp-inspired design
- Green color scheme
- Familiar messaging interface
- Great for users comfortable with WhatsApp

#### 2. **Facebook Messenger Style** (Blue Theme)
- Facebook Messenger-inspired design
- Blue color scheme
- Modern, clean interface
- Professional appearance

#### 3. **Instagram Style** (Dark Theme)
- Instagram-inspired dark mode design
- Purple/dark gradient theme
- Trendy, modern aesthetic
- Appeals to younger audiences

#### 4. **Snapchat Style** (Yellow Theme)
- Snapchat-inspired design
- Yellow color scheme
- Playful, energetic feel
- Casual, friendly appearance

### Template Variations

Each template comes in **4 different layout variations**, showing different conversation states:
- Initial greeting state
- Active conversation with messages
- Button interaction layouts
- Different message bubble arrangements

**How to select:**
- Browse through the template gallery
- Click on any template to preview it
- The selected template will be highlighted
- Your choice affects the overall user experience

---

## Live Preview

**Purpose:** See your changes in real-time before publishing.

The right side panel shows a **live preview** of your chat widget:
- Displays your selected theme
- Shows your logo and colors
- Previews the widget position
- Updates instantly as you make changes

### Preview Elements Shown:
- **Widget Icon:** The floating button users click to open chat
- **Chat Header:** With your logo and branding
- **Message Area:** How conversations will appear
- **Input Field:** Where users type messages

---

## Design Best Practices

> [!TIP]
> **Brand Consistency:** Choose colors and styles that match your existing website design. This creates a seamless user experience and builds trust.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Mobile Responsiveness:** All templates are mobile-responsive, but test your chosen design on different devices to ensure it looks great everywhere.

> [!NOTE]
> **Template vs. Custom:** While templates provide quick, professional designs, you can customize colors within any template to perfectly match your brand.

## Recommended Settings

### For Professional Services (Law, Finance, Consulting)
- **Template:** Facebook Messenger Style (Blue)
- **Position:** Bottom Right
- **Logo:** ON
- **Colors:** Navy blue or professional gray

### For E-commerce & Retail
- **Template:** WhatsApp Style (Green) or Instagram Style
- **Position:** Bottom Right
- **Logo:** ON
- **Colors:** Match your brand colors

### For Creative Agencies & Startups
- **Template:** Instagram Style (Dark) or Snapchat Style
- **Position:** Bottom Right or Left
- **Logo:** ON
- **Colors:** Bold, vibrant brand colors

### For Healthcare & Education
- **Template:** Facebook Messenger Style
- **Position:** Bottom Right
- **Logo:** ON
- **Colors:** Calming blues or greens
