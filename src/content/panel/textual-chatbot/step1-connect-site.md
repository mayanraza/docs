# Step 1: Connect Site (Crawling)

The first step in creating a textual (website) chatbot is to connect your website and allow the system to crawl and learn from your content. This builds the foundation of your chatbot's knowledge base.

---

## Overview

In this step, you'll provide your website URL and select which categories of content the chatbot should learn from. The system will automatically scan your website pages and extract relevant information to train your chatbot.

![Connect Site Step](../../../images/panel.botifire.com/textaual%20cahtbots%20creation%20steps/Step%201%20(crawling).png)

---

## Field Descriptions

### Website URL
**Purpose:** The main URL of your website that you want the chatbot to learn from.

- Enter your complete website URL (e.g., `https://techbackers.io/`)
- The system will use this as the starting point for crawling
- Make sure the URL is accessible and not password-protected

### Scan a Full Page
**Purpose:** Toggle to enable comprehensive scanning of your entire website.

- When enabled, the crawler will scan all linked pages from your main URL
- This provides more comprehensive knowledge for your chatbot
- Recommended for most use cases

## Next Steps

After configuring your website URL and selecting the relevant categories:

1. Click the **Next** button in the bottom-right corner
2. The system will begin crawling your selected content
3. You'll proceed to **Step 2: Greeting Builder** to customize your chatbot's welcome message

---

> [!TIP]
> **Best Practice:** Select only the categories that are relevant to your chatbot's purpose. Too much irrelevant information can dilute the chatbot's effectiveness.

> [!NOTE]
> **Crawling Time:** Depending on your website size and selected categories, the crawling process may take a few minutes. The system will process this in the background as you continue with the next steps.
