# Step 2: Greeting Builder

Customize how your chatbot greets visitors and presents itself. This step allows you to create a welcoming first impression and provide quick access buttons for common queries.

---

## Overview

The Greeting Builder lets you configure your chatbot's welcome message, quick action buttons, company information, and contact details. This is the first interaction users will have with your chatbot, so make it count!

## Buttons

**Purpose:** Quick action buttons that appear in the welcome message for common user queries.

Add buttons that users can click for instant responses to frequently asked questions:

- **FAQ** - Frequently asked questions
- **About Us** - Company information
- **Contact Us** - How to get in touch

**How to add buttons:**
1. Click the **+** button to add more quick action buttons
2. Each button should represent a common user need
3. Limit to 3-5 buttons for best user experience

---

## Company Information

### Company Name
**Purpose:** Your business or brand name that users will see.

- Enter the name you want displayed in the chat widget
- Example: "TechBackers"
- This helps users know who they're chatting with

### Company Industry
**Purpose:** Your business sector or industry classification.

- Select from the dropdown menu (e.g., "Financial & Legal")
- Helps the AI understand context for better responses
- Influences the tone and terminology used

---

## Contact Information

### Contact Number
**Purpose:** A valid phone number for users to reach your business.

- Include country code (e.g., +92)
- Format: +92 - +1 1234567678
- This number may be displayed to users who want direct contact

### Contact Email
**Purpose:** The primary email address for customer support or inquiries.

- Use a monitored email address (e.g., support@example.com)
- This is the best email for customer support
- Users may see this if they need to escalate beyond the chatbot

---

## Social Media Profiles

**Purpose:** Link your social media accounts for users to connect with you on other platforms.

- Click **Add Social Media** to include links to your:
  - Facebook
  - Twitter/X
  - LinkedIn
  - Instagram
  - YouTube
- Helps build trust and provides alternative contact methods

---

## Goodbye Message

**Purpose:** A friendly message when the chat session ends or when users close the widget.

- Example: "If you need anything else, you can come anytime! If you need more assistance, you can send a message anytime. I'm here to help!"
- Encourages users to return
- Leaves a positive final impression
